Tetris on CC3200 Launchpad

Julian Nguyen and Max Molchanov

EEC 172 WQ 2022 Finalist Project

Overview

Our final project for this class is the game Tetris programmed on the TI CC3200 Launchpad.

Startup

After turning on the board and connecting it to the internet, you are greeted with a welcome screen. From here, you can press any control to start the game, and the welcome screen will disappear to make room for the game.

Game Mechanics

The way this works is you control one piece starting from the upper left corner of the board, and it continually falls down until it hits the bottom of the board or another piece. If the game is unable to spawn another piece on the board, the player loses.


Players can clear the board by placing the pieces such that they form a filled up row. These rows will disappear and cause the rows above to fall down and fill the empty space.

Score

The player starts with a score of 0 at the beginning of every game. The score increases when the following events occur:


  • +2: A piece is placed
  • +10: One row is filled

Goal

The goal of this game is to get the highest score on the leaderboard.

High Scores

When you lose the game, you can see the latest high scores downloaded from Amazon Web Services. If your score is a high score, it will be colored red.

Basic Movement

You use these buttons on the remote to manipulate the falling piece:


  • Left: move the falling piece one spot to the left
  • Right: move the falling piece one spot to the right
  • Up: rotate the falling piece clockwise by 90 degrees
  • Down: fast-place the falling piece where it would fall

Pausing

  • Last: pause the game

The player can pause the game, which will clear the board so they can't cheat. To resume the game, they can press LAST again and the board will restore to its original state.

Reset

  • 0: start a new game

When the player starts a new game, the current game is erased.

Cheats

  • 1: cheat mode

The player can press 1 on the remote three times to enable "cheat mode", which allows them to see the board when they pause the game.
