Tetris on CC3200 Launchpad

By Julian Nguyen and Max Molchanov

EEC 172 WQ 2022 Finalist Project

Overview

Our final project is Tetris implemented on the CC3200. We used an Adafruit SSD1351 OLED screen to display the game as well as a TV remote as a controller. When powering up the board, the player is greeted with a welcome screen from which they must press a control to initiate the game (could be any of the registered buttons on the remote). Then the game board is generated and pieces start falling one at a time from the top left corner of the game board. The player must then play the game and try to get the highest score.

Game Rules

The player uses the four arrow buttons on the remote to interact with the falling piece:


  • Left: move the falling piece one spot to the left
  • Right: move the falling piece one spot to the right
  • Up: rotate the falling piece clockwise by 90 degrees
  • Down: fast-place the falling piece where it would fall

As falling pieces begin filling the board, one crucial rule is at play. When a row within the board is filled, it is cleared and all the pieces above it fall down. The objective of the game is to keep the board clear for as long as possible.


The player’s score increases by 2 when a piece is placed and increases by 10 every time one row is filled up.


The game is lost when a piece can not be placed on the board due to the board becoming filled. After a game is lost, the player’s score is saved and persistently stored using Amazon Web Services. A few seconds later, the top five highest scores ever achieved are displayed on the OLED screen next to the game board. If a player gets a score among the top five highest ones, it is highlighted red. The player can then choose to start a new game or disconnect the device.

Pausing

The player can pause the game by pressing LAST on their remote. This clears the board so that the player can't cheat. If the player is ready to resume, they can press LAST again and the board will restore to its original state.

Reset

The player can choose to press 0 on the remote to reset the whole game. This creates a new game for the player starting with a score of zero.

Cheats

The player can press 1 on the remote three times to enable "cheat mode", which lets them see the board when they pause the game.
